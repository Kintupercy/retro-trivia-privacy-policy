Retro Trivia Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024-07-20

1. Introduction

Retro Trivia is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data in our Chrome extension.

2. Information Collection and Use

Retro Trivia does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about its users. The extension only accesses and displays trivia questions from a Google Sheets document.

3. Data Storage

The extension uses Chrome's storage API to store the API key locally on your device. This information is not transmitted to any external servers and is only used to fetch trivia questions.

4. Third-Party Services

Retro Trivia uses the Google Sheets API to fetch trivia questions. No personal information is sent to Google's servers through this process.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at zenfoxdigitalmarketing@gmail.com.
